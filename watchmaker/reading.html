Background Reading

  1. An Introduction to Genetic Algorithms
    Melanie Mitchell
    The definitive introducton to genetic algorithms. An engaging book with a wealth of references.

  2. Introduction to Evolutionary Computing
    A.E. Eiben & J.E. Smith
    Covers a broad range of topics including evolution strategies, evolutionary and genetic programming, and learning classifier systems.

  3. Genetic Programming: An Introduction
    Wolfgang Banzhaf, Peter Nordin, Robert E. Keller & Frank D. Francone
    Tree-based, linear and graph-based genetic programming.

  4. The Blind Watchmaker
    Richard Dawkins
    An accessible introduction to the theory of evolution as it applies to the natural world. Includes Dawkins' accounts of his early experiments with evolutionary computation.

  • Genetic Algorithms and Evolutionary Computation by Adam Marczyk
  • The Hitch-Hiker's Guide to Evolutionary Computation edited by Jörg Heitkötter and David Beasley
  • Genetic Programming as a Means for Programming Computers by Natural Selection (PDF) by John R. Koza
  • A Field Guide to Genetic Programming by Ricardo Poli, William B. Langdon and Nicholas F. McPhee
  • Genetic Argonaut by Marcelo De Brito
